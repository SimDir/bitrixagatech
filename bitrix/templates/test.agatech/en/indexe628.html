Company Brief

Global Tone Communication Technology (Qingdao) Co., Ltd., a wholly-owned subsidiary of Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM), possesses advanced linguistic science and technology and multilingual big data technology. We have established a global multi-language call center and developed a series of language technology products such as JoveTrans, a smart simultaneous translation machine, and YeeBox, a one-click smart translation hardware. We offer big data solutions such as “YeeSight” big data solutions for industry, science and technology and tourism.
