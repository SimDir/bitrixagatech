GTCOM's language and big data technologies support smart tourism

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 17 November 2017views:250

The fourth National Water Conservancy Scenic Area Investment Promotion Conference, hosted by the Organizing Committee of the National Water Conservancy Scenic Area Investment Promotion Conference[YC1] and the China Water Conservancy and Hydropower Market magazine and guided by the Scenic Area Office under the Ministry of Water Resources[YC2], was held on November 16, 2017, at the conference hall of Xiaolangdi Zhongzhou International Hotel, Luoyang, Henan. Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM) was invited as a strategic partner. Based on the theme of innovation, integration and development, the conference aimed to give active play to the role of market mechanism and build a platform for exchanges of construction projects, funds, technologies, equipment and information related to water-conservancy scenic areas.

Speakers at the conference included Song Jianping from Huanghe Xiaolangdi Tourism Development Co., Ltd.[YC1], and Si Yibing, the deputy secretary-general of the Research Institute of Ideological and Political Work of the Ministry of Water Resources[YC2]. Wang Kai, director of the Nanjing Water Affairs Bureau and a member of the National Water Conservancy Scenic Area Review Panel, introduced the path and method of exhibiting water culture in water-conservancy scenic areas. Leaders from--and service providers for--more than 40 scenic areas attended the conference.


Elena Zhang, Vice President of GTCOM

As a guest of the conference, GTCOM Vice President Elena Zhang gave a powerful speech on Language Service and Big Data in Smart Tourism. Emphasizing GTCOM's advantages as a national language-service provider, Vice President Zhang introduced the important roles of multilingual building, language technology, creation of domestic and international publicity platforms, public security and public-opinion analysis in tourism. Further, she elaborated on the importance and standardization of multilingual links in the upgrading of class-A scenic areas. She said that, considering the fact that much more has been lost than has been earned in the evaluation for upgrading of scenic areas, the means of multilingual display needs support from a more professional team. As a language-service provider for the Olympic Games and the World Expo, GTCOM has rich practical experience and is indispensable for language services in all-for-one tourism and smart tourism. GTCOM has a wealth of advantages and development potential in multilingual marketing and publicity, multilingual identification guidance, and intelligent command system. Based on real-life technical capabilities, the company has developed more sophisticated supporting products for smart tourism software. She also modestly invited the participants to visit GTCOM and learn about its culture, which was warmly applauded by the audience.

Ms. Zhang's speech was praised by the participants, and representatives of several scenic areas and equipment manufacturers expressed the wish to engage in cooperation. GTCOM's staff will thus take up the work of establishing relationships and determining needs.


Water-culture exhibition at a water-conservancy scenic area


Given the wide application of information technology in the tourism industry, smart tourism has emerged in response to the times and the public's awareness. Moreover, it's an important way for China's tourism industry and traditional service industries to transform into a modern service sector, while it's also an important support for China in the effort to become not simply a big tourism country but also a strong tourism country. Following this trend, GTCOM will continue to help scenic areas create an intelligent platform for language standardization and a tourism big-data monitoring system, thereby contributing to all-for-one tourism.
