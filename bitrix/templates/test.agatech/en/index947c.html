GTCOM Brings JoveEye to the 10th China Intellectual Property Annual Conference, Its Capabilities of Global Research and Technology Discovery Grab Attention

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 4 September 2019views:7

The 10thChina Intellectual Property Annual Conference (CIPAC2019) was held on September 2 to 3, 2019 at the Hangzhou International Expo Center. The predecessor of this China Intellectual Property Annual Conference is the China Patent Annual Conference. CIPAC2019 covers aspects for the first time including patents, trademarks, operations, services, rights protection and justice. The event, as always, is an industry-recognized annual event for the field of intellectual property. Thus, CIPAC2019 was guided by the National Intellectual Property Administration and the People's Government of Zhejiang Province, sponsored by Intellectual Property Publishing House Co., Ltd. and organized by the Hangzhou Municipal People's Government. It attracted more than 120 IP-related service organizations, 2,000 corporate teams and more than 10,000 representatives of relevant enterprises/organizations around the world. GTCOM was invited to the CIPAC2019, where, at a sub-venue, it hosted an attention-grabbing new-product promotion session based on the theme of "Global Research and Technology Discovery".

▲The 10th China Intellectual Property Annual Conference (CIPAC2019) held at the Hangzhou International Expo Center

▲Wu Xuemin, General Manager of GTCOM's Technological Big Data Unit attending the new-product promotion session of the "Global Research and Technology Discovery"


GTCOM held a new-product promotion session under the theme of "Global Research and Technology Discovery", at which Wu Xuemin, general manager of GTCOM’s Technological Big Data Unit claimed that, for several years, the technology evaluation of technological innovation-based enterprises had been a difficult problem. Because the current big-data applications and intelligent, objective evaluation systems in the field of technology finance aren't fully mature, the development speed of technology finance lags behind the needs of enterprises and society. Thus, it's difficult to scientifically quantify the value of technological achievements (or technological assets), which makes it hard for market players to evaluate technologies on the basis of evidence. The JoveEye Global Research and Technology Discovery System can empower technology finance with technological big data, intelligently discover worthwhile global technological projects, enterprises and talents, and research and determine the proper direction for investment. With its groundbreaking capabilities in technological discovery and evaluation, the system offers auxiliary support for timely decision-making by government bodies and investment institutions. The promotion session attracted considerable attention from representatives of government agencies, enterprises and organizations.

▲GTCOM booth attracts attention from guests

It is worth mentioning that at this conference, GTCOM and its intensive strategic partner CIPRUN jointly released the Brief Report on the Investigation of Patent Operations and Trademark Protection in the Yangtze River Delta Region of 2018. Hangzhou, the place where the CIPAC2019 was held, is one of the most economically developed cities in the Yangtze River Delta region, which is one of the most economically active regions, having the highest degree of openness and the strongest innovation capability in China. Based on the support of the GTCOM's JoveEye (a global technological big-data platform), the brief report analyzed the data on the patent operations in the Yangtze River Delta region from a macro perspective. CIPRUN, as an IP-based technological innovation service platform, provides data intelligence on the trademark protection. The report, as issued by the two parties, drew the attention of all enterprises and organizations present at the event.

▲GTCOM and CIPRUN jointly release the Brief Report on the Investigation of Patent Operations and Trademark Protection in the Yangtze River Delta Region of 2018

At the same time, GTCOM's newborn JoveEye mascot, KeBao, also made its first public appearance, grabbing huge attention from the audience. The guests became fans of the JoveEye mascot KeBao. These numerous fans gathered in front of the booth and participated in the interactive activities to win the special gift KeBao. Moreover, KeBao also had interesting interactions with the mascots of other enterprises/organizations participating in the exhibition.

▲GTCOM's JoveEye mascot,KeBao, makes its debut

With the advance of artificial intelligence and big-data technologies, China's technological innovation industry will usher in new support. Thus GTCOM, as a global leading fintech and research data analytical company, will continue to empower China's innovative technological sector through artificial intelligence and technological big-data technologies. The JoveEye will use artificial intelligence and big-data technologies to deeply explore the innovative application value of technological finance and improve the ability to serve innovative enterprises as the means to create opportunities for continued development in regard to technological innovation industry.
