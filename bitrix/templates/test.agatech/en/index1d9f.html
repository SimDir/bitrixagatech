Faculty and Students of School of Foreign Languages of Qingdao University Visit Qingdao GTCOM Liang Zhenshuang, Deputy General Manager of Qingdao GTCOM, Hired as an Off-campus Tutor

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 14 November 2017views:135

On November 14, Wu Shaoxin, PartySecretary and Mu Xiaoyan, Deputy Secretary of School of Foreign Languages of Qingdao University visited Qingdao GTCOM together with the first batch of undergraduates of “English+Big Data” Innovation Experimental Class and some other postgraduates. Liang Zhenshuang, Deputy General Manager, hired as an off-campus tutor, had in-depth exchanges with the students. Huang Zhen, Deputy Director of R&D Center and his collogues were also present at the meeting.


Talks and Exchanges


Liang Zhenshuang introduced in details YeeSight, the Big Data ecological system, Yeebox, one-touch smart translation hardware, as well as other language technological products, in which the faculty and students showed great interest and took photos of these “blacktechnologies”. Liang Zhenshuang pointed out that the combination of big-data and language technology was an irresistible trend, and cross-language big data was applied to various fields.


Deputy General Manager Liang Zhenshuang is introducing Yeebox, the one-click intelligent translator.


Later at the appointment ceremony, Liang Zhenshuang received the appointment letter from the “English+ Big Data” Innovation Class by Wu Shaoxin, the partysecretary of School of Foreign Languages of Qingdao University and was given the autograph album by the class. Both sides had further discussion on education for interdisciplinary talents in the era of big data. Wu Shaoxin appreciated that Global Tone Communication Technology (Qingdao) Co., Ltd. had been a strong supporter of the college for the new research on language education. He believed the company, which advanced the cross-language big data and language technology, would promote the combination of language technology and big data, helping students better determines the orientation of development and supporting the teaching for adapting to the employment trend closely. In addition, he looked forward to more exchanges between both sides. He hoped that the company would not only boost the teaching of the experimental class, but also serve as a stepping stone for combing other language majors and big data.


Ms. Liang received the appointment letter.


Liang zhenshuang thought that the School of Foreign Languages of Qingdao University, catering to the trend of the national big data development, is farseeing to set up “English+ Big Data” major with special courses. She pointed out that the establishment of the experimental class and the company’s big data projects moved to the same direction and thus the company with experience from cooperating with Xi’an International Studies University and Guangdong University of Foreign Studies, will make a deeper exploration with the School of Foreign Languages to jointly build the project and apply for the research project. Besides, she hoped that students would find the right target, determine their position in the industry chain, instead of fearing the transformation of language industry brought by artificial intelligence. Adding the proficiency of the language, they will make full use of the advantages of big data technology.


Group photo


The faculties and students visited Qingdao GTCOM hired Ms. Liang out an off-campus tutor of the “English+ Big Data” Innovation Class. After jointly building the practice and training base, this is another cooperation which promotes the development of the research project of Qingdao GTCOM. As an enterprise which leads in big data and artificial technology, Qingdao GTCOM will boost the research of language technology and big data to break the border of language and data, and drive the global barrier-free communication and data refinement.
