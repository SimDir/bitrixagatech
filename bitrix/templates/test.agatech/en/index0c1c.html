New office form - LanguageBox leads "smart office"

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 13 June 2019views:17

More than 26 million small and medium-size enterprises in China will experience a smart transformation in 2019, according to Gartner. As the core scenario of enterprise operation, traditional office needs to change due to the influence of the "smart office" concept. Given the constant change of market competition, only by using smart office to improve the office efficiency can enterprises enhance the core aspects of their competitiveness.


How to promote the rapid upgrading of smart office


In the conference room, the key to decision-making is a single hardware product that's compatible with all conference systems. However, many enterprises are beset by the various conference and office equipment brands and incompatibility with various ports. Some employees even say that repeatedly plugging and unplugging office equipment hinders communication and the exchange of knowledge. LanguageBox is designed to adapt to the office environment and flexibly compatible with internal meeting equipment, bringing a new way of smart office.


Compatibility with and connectivity to meeting equipment for smart conferencing

To have a meeting with customers, many enterprises often debug the meeting and office equipment an hour in advance, including microphone, recording pen, projector, TV, recording machine, cable board, etc. Various devices, all with their own specific functions, are connected in a conference room through complicated wiring, which affects the participants' experience.

With LanguageBox, only one HDMI cable is needed to compatibly connect it to the display equipment in the conference room which is thus "transformed" into a "smart conference room" integrating video conferencing, mobile conferencing, smart note-taking and simultaneous interpreting. LanguageBox only needs to be debugged at first use, after which it can be activated directly.

Coherent and traceable to smartly express the thoughts of participants

It is said that after the manuscripts of Three People's Principles were destroyed, Mr. Sun Yat-sen was too busy to write them again. A book was then complied based on the stenographers' notes of a dozen speeches of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. A transcript of Mr. Ren Zhengfei's speech took several stenographers days to put together. This, the importance of note-taking is obvious, as its role isn't limited to the meeting room. It's also a concentrated expression of the information and gist conveyed by the speakers.

Given the importance of note-taking, LanguageBox is powerful in implementing "smart office."With high-quality audio signals and more accurate multilingual meeting information, it can synchronize meeting records at high speed.




LanguageBox can take notes of each speaker's speech, dynamically display subtitles in real time, and intelligently generate meeting recordings and multi-format text files, saving time cost of meeting records and improving overall meeting efficiency. LanguageBox also supports native and external storage, as well as easy saving and exporting of minutes so as to improve the efficiency of minutes reviews and highlights summaries while increasing the value of the smart office.


LanguageBox is compatible with conference and office equipment so as to improve work efficiency, empower meetings and turn ordinary meetings into smart meetings. It effectively completes the extension from the conference room to the office space, makes the smart office a reality and helps enterprises with smart transformation. Thus, it enhances the most important competitive advantages of today's enterprise.
