GTCOM Launches Strategic Cooperation with CUHK

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 24 February 2016views:231

On February 24, 2016, a memorandum of cooperation was signed between Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM), represented by General Manager Eric Yu, and the School of Humanities and Social Science of Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK SZ), represented by Deputy Dean Prof. Wang Lidi, to launch strategic cooperation between the two sides. The agreement was signed in the presence of Huang Youyi, Executive Vice President of the Translators Association of China; Huang Song, General Manager of China Translation & Publishing Corporation (CTPC); Luo Zhiquan, Vice President of CUHK SZ, Prof. Gu Yang, Dean of the School of Humanities and Social Science, Prof. Wang Enmian, and others.

Eric Yu (left) and Wang Lidi (right) signing the memorandum


The two sides also had a strategic seminar and participants had in-depth discussion and reached full consensus on cooperation in regard to machine translation, speech recognition, CAT and big data. In the future, the two sides will carry out comprehensive cooperation for teaching, research and production in regard to language big data, language technology, a joint practical learning base for students, and a joint lab.


The open-access language big data program is a global plan to open data resources in various fields and languages for sharing, gather cross-language big data resources from around the world, and pursue openness, shared benefits, and win-win results. CUHK is a world-renowned university and it has its own big data research center and lab for robot and smart manufacturing, producing world-leading research achievements. The School of Humanities and Social Science has made it clear that is will engage deeply in the Language Big Data Alliance and the open-access language big data program and cooperate extensively in the research of machine translation algorithms and rules.

The two sides discussing possible fields of cooperation


Wang Lidi, Deputy Dean of the School of Humanities and Social Science, CUHK SZ introducing the School


After the meeting, Huang Youyi, Eric Yu and others had exchanges with students of the School of Humanities and Social Science. Huang delivered speech full of vivid examples, while Eric Yu shared his insights in the study of interpreting and the future trends of language technologies, winning rounds of applause.


Huang Youyi, Executive Vice President of the Translators Association of China, giving his presentation


Eric Yu, General Manager of GTCOM, giving his presentation


The strategic cooperation between GTCOM and CUHK is a leap into deeper cooperation between higher education, technology and culture. The joint smart language lab and the open-access language big data program will contribute to the cultivation of high-caliber talents with international vision for the country and region and help promote the reform of the higher education system.
