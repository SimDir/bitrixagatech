YeeFun · The Future! GTCOM's layout in entertainment, AI Video Technology Research Institute unveiled

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 10 December 2018views:118

The inauguration ceremony for the new site of GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment Co., Ltd. and the opening ceremony of the AI Video Technology Research Institute themed "YeeFun · The Future" were held in Laoshan District, Qingdao, on December 8, 2018. The event was attended by Pan Kaixiong, vice president of China Publishing Group Corp.; Li Yan, deputy general manager of China Publishing & Media Holdings Co., Ltd.; Huang Yingsheng, deputy head of Laoshan District, Qingdao; Zhou Qinghua, secretary of the Party Committee and deputy general manager of Zhonghua Book Company; Zhang Jichen, general manager of China National Publications Import & Export (Group) Corporation; and Huang Song, general manager of China Translation Corporation and chairman of Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM).

The inauguration ceremony of the new site of GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment

Leaders from all levels of the Laoshan District Government along with leaders of China Publishing Group Corp., and experts and scholars in the field of AI video technology gathered in the Yongye Building, Laoshan District, Qingdao, at 10 o'clock that morning to witness the inauguration of the new site of GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment. Thus, a new chapter opened in the fields of video entertainment and artificial intelligence.


Huang Yingsheng, deputy head of Laoshan District, Qingdao, delivers a speech


Pan Kaixiong, vice president of China Publishing Group Corp.; Li Yan, deputy general manager of China Publishing & Media Holdings Co., Ltd.; Huang Yingsheng, deputy head of Laoshan District, Qingdao; Zhou Qinghua, secretary of the Party Committee and deputy general manager of Zhonghua Book Company; Zhang Jichen, general manager of China National Publications Import & Export (Group) Corporation; and Huang Song, general manager of China Translation Corporation and chairman of Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM), attended the ceremony. Eric Yu, CEO of GTCOM, and Wang Xiaodong, vice president of GTCOM, attended the event, and Ni Chuchen, general manager of GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment, presided over the event.


At the inauguration ceremony of the new site, Mr. Huang first congratulated the establishment of the AI Video Technology Research Institute on behalf of the Laoshan District Government. He hoped that China Publishing Group Corp., GTCOM and GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment could actively explore the frontiers of science and technology so as to promote the vigorous development of AI video technology and boost economic and social development of Laoshan by technology. He also said that the establishment of the institute will enhance cooperation between universities and enterprises, and upgrade scientific and technological productivity in an effective, targeted and proactive manner. With the strong technical support of GTCOM, the institute is expected to achieve fruitful results in the near future. Laoshan District will continue to support the rapid and high-quality development of GTCOM.


Pan Kaixiong, vice president of China Publishing Group Corp., and

Huang Yingsheng, deputy head of Laoshan District, Qingdao,

jointly unveil the AI Video Technology Research Institute

Li Yan, deputy general manager of China Publishing & Media Holdings Co., Ltd.,

awards appointment letters to experts of the AI Video Technology Research Institute


Mr. Pan Kaixiong and Mr. Huang Yingsheng jointly unveiled the AI Video Technology Research Institute. Mr. Li Yan awarded appointment letters to experts from the institute. At the meeting, Professor Xu Changsheng of the Institute of Automation at the Chinese Academy of Sciences was appointed as the honorary president of the institute, and Professor Zhang Wei of the College of Information Science and Engineering at the Ocean University of China was appointed as the executive vice president. Additionally, experts in the field of AI video technology from the School of Creative Technologies at the University of Portsmouth, the School of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University, the School of Computer & Communication Engineering at China University of Petroleum, the Beijing Key Laboratory of Information Service Engineering at Beijing Union University, the School of Environmental Art at Shanghai Art & Design Academy, the Yangtze River Delta VR Innovation Alliance, and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers were hired as consultants of the institute.

GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment officially settles in Laoshan

The leaders express their affirmation for the development of GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment

The leaders of the Laoshan District Government, China Publishing Group Corp. and GTCOM, amid expectations and blessings, launched the inauguration ceremony of the new site of GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment, thus opening a new chapter for the company.


Eric Yu, CEO of GTCOM, delivers a speech

GTCOM CEO Eric Yu, in his speech, expressed heartfelt thanks to the Qingdao Municipal Party Committee and Government as well as the Laoshan District Committee and Government for their strong support. He stated that GTCOM started with language services and technology but has devoted considerable energy to big data since 2015. Thus, the company has become the industry leader in big-data technology. "Moreover, the development of 5G technology, the entertainment industry has ushered in the era of agglomeration of artificial intelligence. We are working hard to empower the communication of culture and content with big data and artificial intelligence technologies. GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment can take on such a responsibility, which is of extraordinary significance."


Professor Xu Changsheng, honorary president of the AI Video Technology Research Institute, delivers a speech

Professor Xu Changsheng shared his vision for and on behalf of the institute, explaining that there are several factors in the development of artificial intelligence: data, algorithm, computing power and application scenario. The academic circle has advanced algorithms, while the industry has data, computing systems and, most importantly, application scenarios. Only close cooperation and complementary advantages between the two can ensure the maximum value of artificial intelligence. GTCOM has vast data resources and powerful big-data technologies, which are very important for the development of artificial intelligence. Professor Xu said, "We believe that we can produce internationally influential products and platforms in the identification, dissemination and optimization of intelligent video by combining the advanced algorithms of the academic circle."


The first seminar of the AI Video Technology Research Institute


The experts from the institute then held the first seminar at the new site of GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment, where they eagerly discussed how the powerful copyright resources and content output channels held by GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment could be extended to the realm of AI technology. Professor Xu Changsheng said that, given GTCOM Digital Media & Entertainment's current copyright video resource advantages and business plan, the research of the joint laboratory should be taken as an academic position while the national application should be related to customer needs and research effort should be devoted to the business model as the means to pursue common development.
