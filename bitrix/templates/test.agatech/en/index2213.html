Resonate with the World: GTCOM Brings YeeSight to GMIC

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 18 November 2016views:231

The Global Mobile Internet Conference (GMIC) was held in Bangalore, India, during November 16-17. It is a milestone event for the mobile Internet industry worldwide. The Bangalore session of GMIC has become the most influential event in India and even the world for the mobile Internet industry. Themed “Resonate with the World”, the event explored the present and the future of mobile Internet ecosystems in India. It attracted more than 6,000 participants, including senior executives from Flipkart, Ola, Snapdeal, Hike and other top-notch Indian companies, as well as word-leading enterprises like Google, Facebook, Baidu, Xiaomi, and Cheetah Mobile. Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM) was invited to the event and Yan Guoqing, Director for International Operation, delivered a speech titled “AI +Machine Translation & Big Data, Globalization Enabler for Business”.





GTCOM is currently building the language capacity layer, big data analysis capacity layer, and cognitive intelligence capacity layer for companies all over the world. With its YeeCloud language service ecosystem centered on machine translation and cross-language big data analysis platform, GTCOM provides advanced and innovative services to users all over the world.


YeeSight is a cross-language big data analysis platform. It employs the most advanced natural language processing technology of the world, digs deep into scientific computing of structured and unstructured data, gives full play to the advantages of machine translation and human translation, provides accurate, fast, visualized and customized services, gather information in all languages, of all times, and from all regions of the world, and offers professional solutions for global customers.



GTCOM entered into strategic cooperation with the host in April at GMIC Beijing. Technology changes life and data creates value. In the future, GTCOM will provide global enterprises and users with more advanced intelligent machine translation technology, a better cross-language big data analysis platform, and provide full support for enterprises to connect with the world in the DT time with technology and innovation.


[About GMIC]


Global Mobile Internet Conference (GMIC) is a global event of the mobile Internet industry hosted by the Great Wall Society (GWS). It has developed into the largest international event of the mobile Internet industry with attended by top-notch guests. It is an annual milestone for the industry. First held in 2009, GMIC has welcomed nearly one million participants in total from more than 70 countries around the world. Starting from Beijing, GMIC has so far reached out to the Silicon Valley, Tokyo, Bangalore, Tel Aviv, Jakarta, Sao Paulo, and Hong Kong.
