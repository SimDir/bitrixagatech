Elena Zhang, Vice-President of GTCOM: The AI big-data platform already has strong abilities in regard to intelligent analysis and trend forecasting

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 8 December 2017views:288

On December 6, Elena Zhang, Vice-President of Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM), presented the YeeSight big-data platform at the launching meeting of innovative products in the Fourth World Internet Conference. She used several practical cases to vividly introduce the powerful ability of the big-data platform to intelligently collect global data, analyze emotional data, predict event development trends, and visualize data in the Internet age.


Elena Zhang, Vice-President of GTCOM, presented the YeeSight big-data platform


Debut of YeeSight at the artificial intelligence event, attracting much attention

Elena Zhang mentioned that this year's Wuzhen World Internet Conference is a seminar about "the future development trend of artificial intelligence." Tim Cook, CEO of Apple Inc.; Ma Yun, Chairman of the Board of Alibaba Group; Ma Huateng, Chairman of the Board of Tencent; Li Yanhong, Chairman and CEO of Baidu; Robert Kahn, the father of the Internet; Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google Inc., and other industry leaders delivered colorful speeches on the development of artificial intelligence at the conference. As one of the world's 411 enterprises invited to the "Light of the Internet," and the only enterprise engaged in language + big data + artificial intelligence + Internet, GTCOM will show the value and charm of AI big data through the YeeSight big-data platform independently developed by GTCOM using AI technology.


Cross-language intelligent search + hundreds of billions of knowledge graphs = intelligent collection and analysis of global data

According to Elena Zhang, GTCOM has built the YeeCloud language-technology ecosystem and the YeeSight big-data ecosystem through continuous R&D and investment in advanced technologies of machine translation, speech recognition, image recognition, semantic search, knowledge graph, big-data analysis and big-data visualization. GTCOM has also set up the 2020 Cognitive Intelligence Research Institute, focusing on the study of new-generation technologies in areas such as artificial intelligence and cognitive science. Under the YeeSight big-data ecosystem, GTCOM has launched big-data solutions and big-data analysis platforms for different vertical industries, such as Insider standard big data, JoveBird financial big data, and public security big data, serving governments, enterprises, news media, financial investment institutions and other industry users worldwide.


Elena Zhang, Vice-President of GTCOM


The YeeSight big-data platform embodies the application value of the company's language technologies and cognitive intelligence research results, and provides basic platform support for the development and application of AI big data. Given the keyword-based cross-language search and other language technology advantages, the YeeSight big-data platform can break time and space limitations and overcome language barriers to have real-time access to data information in 65 languages worldwide, with data sources covering more than 200 countries and regions. Thanks to the technologies of intelligent semantic search and the knowledge-graph generation based on neural networks, the YeeSight big-data platform can update 30 million articles of news page data and more than 500 million articles of social data per day.


Multidimensional algorithm model → Visualized display of "emotional data"

The development of AI technologies makes human beings more creative and machines more valuable. Elena Zhangelaborated on the capabilities of YeeSight big data in intelligent analysis and visualized display of emotional data using practical cases. She explained that GTCOM has independently developed multidimensional algorithm models, one of which is the industry-specific neural network algorithm. This algorithm can extract "emotive factors" contained in the obtained data and vividly display the proportion of positive versus negative public sentiment through such forms as technology life curves and ECGs. Elena Zhangstressed that driven by AI technologies, the big-data platform has been able to "read" people's emotional tendencies to some extent, and possesses strong intelligent analysis and trend-forecasting capabilities.

The future is an age in which big data and artificial intelligence are converging and evolving. It is also an age when we change our thinking patterns, break the constraints of time, space and language, achieve cross-industry integration, and jointly see the role of hundreds of millions of data around the world. As the world's leading big data and artificial intelligence company, and the world's leading enterprise having hundreds of millions of high-quality vertical industry corpus resources and advanced language technologies, GTCOM is committed to realizing the goal of "Language Connects the World, and Data Inspires the Future," giving industry users worldwide more intelligent, professional big-data solutions, and moving forward in order to lead the AI big-data industry in the Internet age.


Site | Light of the Internet Expo: Highlight of GTCOM

In addition to releasing the YeeSight big-data platform at the launching meeting of innovative products in the Fourth World Internet Conference, GTCOM also presented its big-data solutions and big-data products for different industries at the Light of the Internet Expo, which astonished the domestic and foreign guests alike.



Opening ceremony of the Light of the Internet Expo, the Fourth World Internet Conference

Chinese and foreign guests are experiencing the YeeSight big-data analysis platform


As an important functional section of the Fourth World Internet Conference, the Light of the Internet Expo, based on the theme "Developing Digital Economy for Opening and Sharing: Working Together to Build a Community with a Shared Future for Cyberspace," displayed the innovative applications of cloud computing, the Internet of Things, artificial intelligence and other global Internet technologies as well as the latest achievements in the development of digital economy, from the perspectives of "internationalization, innovation, future, leader, and integration."
