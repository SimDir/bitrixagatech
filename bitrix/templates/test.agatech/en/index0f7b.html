GTCOM Attends the 2014 Annual Meeting of China National Committee for Translation and Interpreting Education

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 29 March 2014views:230

On March 29, 2014, the 2014 annual meeting of China National Committee for Translation and Interpreting Education was held at Hebei Normal University and present at the event were over 300 MTI teachers from more than 150 renowned higher education institution in China. At the meeting, Lv Jing, Deputy Managing Director of Global Tone Communication Technology Co. Ltd. (GTCOM), introduced the United Nations Language Professional Training Programme (UNLPP) and the efforts made by China Translation and Publishing Corporation (CTPC) and GTCOM for the cultivation of translation and interpreting professionals and the cooperation between education instuttions and enterprises.


At the meeting


Given the rapid socioeconomic development in China, CTPC joined hands with CIFAL Shanghai under the United Nations Institute for Training and Research (UNITAR) to launch the United Nations Language Professional Training Programme in order to meet the urgent need for high-caliber international language talents, and adapt to the differentiated market demand for application-oriented, professional and specialized international language talents, push for the establishment of a training and certification system for language professionals, which connects well to its international counterparts, and give full play to the strong facilitating function of language in global arena. Meanwhile, supported by the advantageous resources of UNITAR and China Publishing Group, a programme office (China) and an expert panel were also put in place for planning, implementation, and promotion.


Lv Jing, GTCOM Deputy Managing Director, presenting UNLPP


UNLPP covers the six sectors of current affairs, politics, finance, law, society, and culture; reaches out into a number of popular industries; and offers tests at three levels, P1, P2, and P3. It is currently the international language professional certification test of the widest coverage and the highest standard in the field of language service-related training. Therefore, it is highly valuable.


Meanwhile, YeeCloud, a platform jointly built by CTPC and GTCOM, will present a global translation expert database and a cloud language database to gather and provide more talents for the language service industry. Building the first cloud language service platform, CTPC and GTCOM wish to join hands with more specialized higher education institutions for better cooperation between the industry and teaching and research institutions, so as to contribute more to the language service industry of China and the world.


Master of Translation and Interpreting programs were first launched in China in 2007 when it was approved by the Academic Degree Committee of the State Council to train high-caliber, application-oriented and specialized talents for translation and interpreting. CTPC has been in cooperation with many universities that offer MTI programs for years and it currently serves as practical training bases for Peking University, Beijing Foreign Studies University, Beijing Language and Culture University, University of International Business and Economics, China Foreign Affairs University, University of International Relations, Beijing International Studies University, China University of Petroleum, and Zhejiang University. With YeeCloud launched, CTPC will be able to provide more extensive training materials, real-life scenarios, and more reliable tests for students within the framework of university-enterprise cooperation, making its contribution to the cultivation of translation and interpreting talents.


GTCOM at the event


UNLPP was warmly welcomed by college teachers attending the meeting. Many of them asked for more information or for more contact points so that they can ask for details of the programme later. Many also expressed intentions for cooperation with CTPC and GTCOM. Coincidentally, the first certification test of UNLPP was completed on the day of the meeting in Beijing (Beijing International Studies University), Heilongjiang (Heilongjiang University), Jilin (Jilin University), Liaoning (Dalian University of Foreign Languages), and Wuhan (Wuhan University). About this, head of the UNLPP management office said that in the future, UNPLL tests will be carried out in more than 40 cities all over China and it was hoped that UNLPP could be connected with MTI programs so as to train more translation and interpreting talents for our country.


[About CTPC]

China Translation and Publishing Corporation (CTPC) was established in March 1973 as the United National Document Group and renamed Beijing Translation and Publishing Office in April 1976 as approved by the State Council. Then in August 1978, it changed to its current name as approved by the State Council. It has successively been put under the administration of National Publishing Administration, the General Administration of Press and Publication, and China Publishing Group. It is an important member of the Translators Association of China.


[About GTCOM]

Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM) is a controlled subsidiary of China Translation and Publishing Corporation (CTPC). Carrying forward the high service quality which is a tradition of CTPC over its history of 40 years, GTCOM delivered services to the Olympics Games held in Beijing in 2008, the Shanghai World Expo of 2010, the Asian Youth Games in Nanjing and various other major national-level events. Also, it has been providing language services to the United Nations and many other organizations and individuals for years as a national-level language service provider.


GTCOM pools advantageous resources from China Publishing Group Corp. and China Translation and Publishing Corporation to build YeeCloudTM with world-leading speech information technology and Internet and mobile Internet technologies. YeeCloudTM includes three major platforms: the multilingual cloud call center and video conference platform, the multilingual information analysis and management platform, and the cloud language education platform; and two big data resource banks: the cloud language resource database and the global translation expert database. Adopting new service delivery models such as multilingual cloud call center and video conference, online delivery, mobile Internet terminal application, etc., YeeCloud allows global users to get connected via any terminal from any place and at any time and enjoy smart, integrated, customized and open cloud language services.
