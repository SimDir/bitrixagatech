GTCOM Wins Best Multi-Language Call Center Award 2014

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 30 October 2014views:230

The 10th China Contact Center Industry Summit and the China Best Call Center Award Granting Ceremony was held in Beijing on October 29, 2014. The event gathered representatives from telecommunication, banking, insurance, securities, e-commerce, tourism, mobile phone, home appliance, training, translation and publishing, outsourcing, and technical manufacturing enterprises; over 100 guests from the contact center association; and dozens of experts from Asia-Pacific, Europe and America for exchanges, experience sharing, and cooperation. Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM) participated in the event as invited and was awarded Best Multilingual Call Center of China Award.

At the event

Fourth from left in the upper photo and second from left in the lower photo is Ms. Elena Zhang receiving the award on behalf of GTCOM


Themed on reshaping the value of contact centers, the event focuses on industries, embraces changes, looks into the future, and mainly discusses the position and value of contact centers in an enterprise in a time of changes. During the event, a number of renowned Chinese companies were granted the Best Call Center of China Award, which is led by China National Contact Center and CRM Association (CNCCA) and supported by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology. This is the only important annual award of the call center industry in China.

The call center industry started in China in mid-1990s and has now developed to a considerable scale after more than a decade of efforts. Call centers are present in many different industries and covers a wide range of businesses. However, this industry, like many other industries, is now undergoing profound changes following the shifts in the way customers communicate, the Internet-based way of thinking, social media, and the consumption habits of the new generation. In such a background, innovative call centers are pioneers in reshaping the value of contact centers.

GTCOM YeeCloud multi-language cloud call center is a comprehensive language service platform built with smart speech technology and advanced Internet technology for telephone interpretation, video conference interpretation and online translation. It allows users to get timely, affordable, and all embracing language service via call center, video conference, mobile app, email, fax, etc. With a multi-language cloud call center in place, individual users may get access to translation services in real time by simply making a call. The call center will help them with cross-language communication in scenarios such as tourism, shopping, accommodation, catering, medical services, personal phone calls, hospitality services, taxi hailing, etc. Enterprises may get languages services via a number of different terminals for business meetings, telephone conferences, international negotiations, conference and exhibition affairs, virtual offices, and call center outsourcing.

GTCOM YeeCloud multi-language cloud call center not only provides the most comprehensive services in the most advanced delivery model, but also fills the blank of a multi-language call center in China. Since the platform was launched in 2008, it has been put into high-level use in the banking, securities, insurance, trust, telecommunication, automobile, IT, manufacturing, aviation, e-commerce, outsourcing, Internet, and various other industries. As a green hand in the industry, the multi-language cloud call center has been warmly welcomed and won a number of awards. This award represents another recognition for the innovative spirit from the call center industry.

In the future, YeeCloud multi-language cloud call center will continue to innovate amid changes in the industry and create more value for customers.

[About CNCCA]

China Call-Center & CRM Association (CNCCA) is a voluntary organization formed by operators, manufacturers, service providers, and users related to call centers, customer relationship management, and telephone customer services, and all enterprises, public institutions and individuals that care about the industry in China. It is in the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and is managed by China Electronics Chamber of Commerce. It is the only association of the call center and CRM industry approved by the Ministry of Civil Affairs. It is not for profit.

The association organizes seminars, training sessions, exhibitions, tours, and visits every year so as to provide members with a platform for exchanges, help them update their management practices and technical tools, and improve their operation and service quality. Meanwhile, it also grants authoritative awards every year to promote benign competition between members and polish the image of the industry.


[About GTCOM]

Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM) is a controlled subsidiary of China Translation & Publishing Corporation (CTPC). Carrying forward the high service quality which is a tradition of CTPC over its history of 40 years, GTCOM delivered services to the Olympics Games held in Beijing in 2008, the Shanghai World Expo of 2010, the Asian Youth Games in Nanjing and various other major national-level events. Also, it has been providing language services to the United Nations and many other organizations and individuals for years as a national-level language service provider.

GTCOM pools advantageous resources from China Publishing Group Corp. and China Translation & Publishing Corporation to build YeeCloudTM with world-leading speech information technology and Internet and mobile Internet technologies. YeeCloudTM is included in the reform and development project bank of the State Administration of Press, Publishing, Radio, Film and Television and is funded by the national cultural industry fund. It brings together translation talents and language resources from around the world and adopts advanced technologies such as natural language processing and deep neural network. Adopting new service delivery models such as multilingual cloud call center and video conference, online delivery, mobile Internet terminal application, etc., YeeCloud allows global users to get connected via any terminal from any place and at any time and enjoy smart, integrated, customized and open cloud language services.
