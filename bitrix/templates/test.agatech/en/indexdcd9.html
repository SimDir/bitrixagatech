Big Data Solutions

Financial IndustryScience & TechnologyGovernments and EnterprisesManufacturingIndustriesTourism

Introduction

Equipped with IoT, big data and AI, manufacturing big data solutions could enable the industries to conduct intelligent fault warning and diagnosis, remote operation and control of products, etc.


Pain Spots of the Industry

For Manufacturing Businesses

Outdated after-sales service model, high cost, low efficiency and base customer satisfaction. Product innovation runs into bottlenecks, Intensified market competition, urgent need to increase value-added services. Progressively higher requirements for user service.

For Client Factory

Time-consuming repair after equipment failure incurs considerable economic loss. Exponential increase of labor cost and difficulties to recruit and retain labor. Equipment maintenance teams are not professional enough, and it is difficult to accumulate experience due to high personnel mobility. The management cannot obtain information on the factory's production in a timely manner.

Solutions

Applications

  • Reduce costs and increase efficiency
  • Through unified monitoring, remote diagnosis and distant operation and maintenance of the equipment, the efficiency of equipment operation and maintenance is improved along with lowered cost of after-sales service. The revenue generated by value-added services is increased by providing big data added services.
  • Facilitate product development and innovation
  • The collection of huge volumes of data generated by the equipment in the operation process enables the production of more analyzed data for the manufacturing companies' product innovation. The platform conducts real-time monitoring and analysis of the latest global scientific research and expert papers in the industry, and provides powerful technical support for product innovation.
  • Accelerate the transformation and upgrading of manufacturing businesses
  • The big data platform for smart operation and maintenance improves the companies’ after-sales service capabilities, enhances the market competitiveness of their products and services, promotes their innovation, thus assisting the companies in their effort to transform and upgrade from “manufacturing” to “manufacturing + service”.
  • Fuel innovation and upgrading in the industry
  • As an important carrier for digital, Internet-based intelligent transformation of the manufacturing industry, the big data platform for smart operation and maintenance will boost the overall improvement of the industry’s competitiveness in the entire process and all aspects, and facilitates the industry’s innovation and upgrading, to transform and fuel China's manufacturing industry.

Cases

  • The platform has successfully completed the installation of big data platforms for smart operation and maintenance for several companies under CHTC, and has been expanded and further developed in Haier Group and Sinopec.
