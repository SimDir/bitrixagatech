Technology Explosion: A close look at the JoveEye sales team during the Technology Festival

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 6 June 2019views:19

More than half a month has passed since the May 18th launch of the Technology Festival under the banner of "The Technology Explosion." The team members of the JoveEye technology big-data product line have worked together to bring JoveEye closer to users and help enterprise customers to explore the future of technology with technology.



At the leading end of the work chain, the JoveEye e-commerce sales team, which introduced the Technology Big Data Platform to users, has spent each day at the forefront of the Technology Festival, energizing the proceedings with its expertise. Their patience, professionalism and enthusiasm have allowed JoveEye Technology Big Data to reach more enterprise users.

Recently, I had an interview with the JoveEye e-commerce sales professionalin order to obtain an understanding of this young, positive, energetic,motivated sales team.



Optimistic: Wang Yangfeng


Wang Yangfeng is a constant optimist and team participant. At work, though, he is serious, rigorous, helpful and efficient in managing his own emotions. During the chat, he impressed me with his skill as a communicator. In an exchange with a customer --called Mr. A--the customer initially objected. However, he then became interested and ultimately expressed the desire to experience JoveEye immediately. Wang Yangfeng conveyed the power and value of communication to that customer.

He said it's important to understand an enterprise user's actual needs through communication and then introduce JoveEye's core functions such as deep evaluation by AI algorithms, precise cross-language semantic retrieval and multidimensional scientific research data analysis, all of which will tell the user how to empower the enterprise's development with technology. Thus, it's his task to recommend this AI big-data product to more enterprise users so as to enhance their research efficiency and reduce risks. It's also a small but practical effort to help build an innovative country.

Wang said that practice has given him the ability to switch between "serving" and "served" roles in work and life. He enjoys the working mode and state of communicating with people online.



Fledgling: Chen Xiangfei


Chen Xiangfei, who will soon graduate, has seen her internship enriched through experience with big data and artificial intelligence. As a novice, she is enterprising and energetic. "My job involves dealing with clients from all walks of life and answering professional questions," she said. "Although some profound technical questions might not be answered perfectly, my experience clearly shows that I'm growing rapidly. I've accumulated some industry knowledge and working skills with which to explore new industries and fields and energize my endeavors."

She said that although she is a beginner with no prior work experience, she is enthusiastic and a quick learner. In the coming days, she'll make greater efforts to consult with her senior colleagues and learn more about communication methods, customer-demand mining and the core advantages of technology big data so as to plan a good start for her career development and accumulate core strength for her future career.




Good at thinking and summarizing: Yang Wentao


"Calm, steady and responsible with a big-picture view" is the first impression Yang Wentao left on me. During our conversation, this engaging young man shared thoughts and insights on his work.

In words full of admiration, he introduced his teammates' many excellent qualities. For example, he said "A" has professional customer communication skills, "B" has outstanding team management ability, and "C" is good at solving difficult problems... Teammates have always inspired him to learn more. He has the resilience needed to persevere despite any setback. He told me that either one successful contract signed for 1,000 people or no successful contract signed for 1,000 people is a win for him. It may seem like no gain at the moment, but in the long run, every communication can lead to exciting new opportunities.

He loves thinking and summing up. I believe this young man, who is not only awed by his work but also full of courage and resilience will achieve impressive growth as part of the JoveEye e-commerce team.

In the future, he'll try his best to introduce JoveEye to more enterprise users so that more enterprises can ride the express train of AI big-data development. In that way, he can empower the innovative development of enterprises.



Dare to challenge: Wang Run


Wang Run is a clever, confident girl born after 1995. Despite her delicate appearance, she has a strong mind that dares to challenge and is resolute in the face of defeat. She told me that sales work requires not only a tolerant and magnanimous mind but also a strong heart.

In Wang's view, a practitioner in the sales industry, whether offline or online, must be an agile thinker with the ability to organize language, communicate intelligently and manage issues in business terms. The challenge of giving an appropriate, professional answer to any tricky question makes her work more exciting and inspires her to achieve progress.

Descriptions of the above four colleagues outline the portrait of GTCOM's JoveEye e-commerce sales team. We can see their vitality, courage and resilience, reflecting the team's high-spirited temperament and spirit.
