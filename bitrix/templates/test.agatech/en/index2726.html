Big Data Solutions

Financial IndustryScience & TechnologyGovernments and EnterprisesManufacturingIndustriesTourism

Introduction

The industrial big data solution applies GTCOM's advanced huge data technology to the various aspects of industry, including finance, production, management, procurement, logistics, sales, marketing and after-sales service, to equip the large data system with the modes and results of such smart functions as description, diagnosis, forecasting, decision making and control. With customized solutions, enterprises can open up end-to-end full business processes and rapidly shape a complete digital ecosystem, effectively drawing strengths from business insights and suggestions on decision making.

Features

Acquisition Technology

With a core advantage in acquisition technology, we independently develop Internet information collection tools.

Natural Language Processing

We have formed technical advantages of machine translation and language big data analysis and processing in the field of industry.

Asset Management System

Through data collection along with in-depth mining and analysis, our platform provides staunch support for enterprises to achieve smart manufacturing and upgrade their marketing. We facilitate industrial companies in their effort to reduce inventory, lower costs, promote internal R&D and management innovation and enhance competitiveness.

Visual Report Display

Our platform can collect data of multiple languages from various sources and the capability for multilingual, machine translation, business intelligence analysis. With many data sources and interfaces, the platform enables rapid visual report display based on the Paas platform and provides customized industrial big data solutions that cater to the needs of clients.

Applications

  • IaaS Business Intelligence System
  • (1) Data collection capability: global, multilingual and real-time; (2) Data classification: classification according to such factors as field and time; (3) Personalized recommendation: personalized recommendation based on user interest revealed by background data.
  • IaaS Intelligent Analysis System
  • Multiple-intelligent, analysis models; Quick search of information; Report generated with one click; Export in multiple formats (Word, Excel, PDF);
  • IaaS Monitoring and Forecasting System
  • (1) Visual display and visual presentation: It supports all major forms of analysis and operation; (2) Multi-platform display: seamless support for the monitoring and forecasting on PC, iPhone and Android; (3) Data prediction through a deep analysis algorithm to produce data correlation insights.
  • DaaS Data Interface Service
  • Information database, Enterprise database, Business database, Research database, Desensitized database

Cases

  • Smart Supply Chain Whole Process Sales and Operation Planning System

  • The data service platform for product price monitoring uses data as the basis for sales, realizes digital operation of product, enhances the product market response through data and improves the capability for sales.
  • The platform can promptly detect malpractices in the market such as cross-region sales and counterfeit goods to protect the interests of the enterprises. It can grasp information related to sales demands and trends, monitor the behaviour of competitors and formulate corresponding market strategies in a timely manner.

  • Grid Management Big Data System

  • The grid management big data system enables the display and closed-loop tracking of market grid information. The market, which used to be managed according to people or clients, is now managed according to grids of administrative regions. The market has also shifted its focus from sales and revenue to retail and grid share. Grid management is realized through a geological perspective. The system helped Haier Group complete its global grid data management and control, access and accommodate7 types of databases, and achieve PB-level data processing and visual exploration analysis. It can be applied to domestic and global marketing data management of large enterprises.

  • Big Data System for Global Competitive Product Analysis

  • As one of the core components of the company’s great data platform. The large data system for universal advantage product conducts comparative analysis of the company’s 500 product lines and 2,000 similar leading products. It conducts user portraits for the company’s 18 million registered users and produces a monthly visual data analysis report for the purchase department to achieve accurate marketing. The system, based on the YEE-MPP framework independently developed by GTCOM, accesses all the group's raw market data and possesses PB-level data processing capabilities. It can provide promotional big data analysis service for domestic and foreign companies, and assist the sales and strategy departments of the company in order to understand the ever-changing international market trends, analyze field dynamics and discover and seize market growth opportunities.

  • Big Data Analysis Platform for Global Procurement System

  • The platform establishes unified standards for universal procurement data and models for cost analysis: regional analysis, category analysis, supplier analysis, price analysis, supplier analysis, opportunity analysis; It performs the function of decision making, management and analysis. With the visual display of the whole procurement process, it ensures information transparency of internal and external data integration. As China's first international big data system for multilingual business procurement, the platform realizes connectivity and sharing of the global procurement system data, real-time business data display, achieves the generic use of materials and continuously improves cost competitiveness. The platform system involves data collection of various business systems in 13 regions around the world, six kinds of source system languages, about 30,000 suppliers, about 62 million orders yearly and more than 400 corporate users.
