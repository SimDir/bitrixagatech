CTC Joins Hands with GDUFS

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 27 March 2016views:231

On March 27, 2016, an agreement for strategic cooperation was signed in Beijing between China Translation Corporation (CTC), represented by General Manager Huang Song, and Guangdong University of Foreign Studies (GDUFS), represented by President Zhong Weihe. The two sides will launch in-depth strategic cooperation in the fields of cross-language big data think tank, machine translation, speech recognition, and CAT, to create a model project of cooperation between production, teaching and research institutions.


Signing the agreement


According to Huang Song, CTC, a leading enterprise in the industry, will do all it can to promote in-depth collaborative innovation with GDUFS in terms of technological resources, platform building, etc., and create a brand new cooperation model of language + big data + strategic alliance. Zhong Weihe agreed with Huang and said that by launching cooperation with CTC, GDUFS aimed to further improve its teaching, research and innovation, as well as its capacity to benefit society; and that the cooperation would build an innovation platform that was collaborative and open for sharing, which was highly significant.

[About CTC and GTCOM]

China Translation Corporation (CTC), previously China Translation & Publishing Corporation, is a national-level translation and publishing institution established with the approval of the State Council. It is the only Chinese language service provider for the United Nations and has served as language service provider for the 2008 Olympic Games in Beijing, the Shanghai World Expo, and many other important events. Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM) is a controlled subsidiary of CTC. It makes use of natural language processing and big data technologies and the language big data and service experience accumulated over more than 40 years to build the YeeSight big data analysis platform, YeeCloud language service platform, and YeeKit professional tool platform. It is a world-leading cross-language big data service provider.
