JoveTrans

Introduction

JoveTrans, Always have you understood

JoveTrans is an AI-powered smart voice recorder and translation device. It employs the world's leading artificial intelligence voice technology and supports simultaneous interpretation in 35 languages, including Chinese, English, Russian and French. The accuracy of interpretation reaches 97%. JoveTrans allows cross-region, cross-language and multi-machine building of groups for free talking as well as real-time interpretation of HD recordings.


Capability

Simultaneous Translation in 35 Languages

JoveTrans provides simultaneous translation, with an accuracy of 97%, in 35 languages: Chinese, Polish, English, Arabic, Danish, German, Spanish, Finnish, French, Italian, Thai, Japanese, Korean, Dutch, Portuguese, etc.

Note-taking with Smart Recording

Transnational Intercommunication and Group Chat

Invite other users to the chat channel with online request code and start conversations in multi-machine groups to engage in transnational and trans-regional communication.

Application in Multiple Scenarios

Multilateral conference, overseas travel, interview, front desks of hotels and airports, study abroad, self-learning at home.
