A Language Asset Tool Platform Powered by Big Data

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 30 July 2014views:230


In 2014, big data turned from a concept into real application and it pushed various industries onto the Internet for leapfrog development. Now we can see an outburst of business opportunities and value triggered by big data. As more and more data-based business model are created, big data will be applied in more and more sector of society, including finance, manufacturing, energy, trade, logistics, agriculture, and language services.


In this information age where speed is everything, human translation can no longer meet the huge market demand for fast translation due to its low productivity and high cost.


Over the years, the translation service industry has gone through changes and service providers have strived to align themselves to international convention and meet the evolving needs of all parties involved. Customers want more work done within a shorter time and a higher quality; translators and experts want to collect the scattered resources, have more materials to learn from, and have access to translation tools. Language service, as a traditional industry, is now moving forward with time and embracing new technologies such as big data and cloud computing. This is the path towards a better future.


Pushed by the demand of various parties in the market and emerging technologies, traditional language services and products evolved and a number of new products came into being, powered by new language processing technologies. For example, Google launched the new technology that translates texts in images and Skype now allows real-time chatting in multiple languages. Also riding this tide, YeeKit is an open language asset tool platform.


YeeKit distinguishes itself from other similar products with efficient resource integration supported by core technologies such as big data, cloud computing, and deep learning, and it, therefore, solves the problem of data island that has long been a bottleneck in the improvement of business efficiency. YeeKit is an open and systematic translation tool kit for translators and language enthusiasts. It puts together the multiple functions and features of various single-purpose language service products to present a full set of tools free of charge and offer open access to resources, so that language services can be more efficient and conveniently available to meet the demand for faster, more efficient and better translation services, pushing the language service industry forward.


YeeKit provides the four major functions of machine translation, computer-aided translation, parallel text, and language asset management and it shows the following four special features:


1. Field-specific multi-language machine translation


YeeKit's machine translation is field-specific and specialized big data improves translation quality. Meanwhile, it allows users to modify machine translation and it will automatically learn from what the users put in to continuously improve the quality of machine translation.

2. Open access to language assets


Parallel texts, term banks, and translation memory are all language assets that need to be managed. Such data can be found everywhere on the Internet but they are usually scattered and hard to use effectively. YeeKit pools all kinds of language resources and conduct professional processing to ultimately form and professional corpus with hundreds of millions of entries.


Meanwhile, following the trend of language asset sharing, YeeKit created the first resource sharing and exchange platform for language big data. Users may use their own language assets online to improve translation efficiency or upload and share them for exchange. YeeKit also offers tools to manage, retrieve, share, exchange and evaluate language asset.


3. Open-access computer-aided translation (CAT)


CAT tools offered by YeeKit is neither a simple machine translation tool nor a human translation platform. It allows both humans and the machine to participate in the work on a project so that translators or students can work less on the documents and deliver the translations more effortlessly with higher efficiency and quality. While doing translation and managing translation projects online with the tool, users may also use open-access language resources online to improve translation quality.


4. Parallel texts


The parallel text function of YeeKit gathers similar sentences to help professional translators better under stand the original text and the translations or to help students learn.


Today, in an age of the Internet, resource sharing and user-generated content are the trend. YeeKit is a technological innovation that caters to the needs of the language service industry. The language service industry should make better use of Internet technologies to seek breakthroughs in business model and technological capacity, be brave to innovate, and pursue better development by better meeting customers' needs.
