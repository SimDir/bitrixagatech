Plaque Unveiled for China Translation Research Center

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 26 August 2014views:230

On August 25, China Translation Research Center was formally launched in the coastal city of Qingdao. It is created by China Publishing Group Corp. by pooling translation-related resources from home and abroad. The launching event was welcomed Vice President Li Yan of China Publishing Group Corp., General Manager Lin Guofu of China Translation and Publishing Corporation, over 30 renowned sinologists, translators, educators, diplomats and scholars, as well as nearly 100 distinguished guests from home and abroad.


As Li Yan, Vice President of China Publishing Group, said in his speech, as China rises economically, the Chinese culture will also march into the world and become a hotspot; so the Chinese government has made it clear that we should increase communication with foreign countries, build our capacity of international communication and create a better system to have our voice heard abroad. This is why China Translation Research Center is established.



Li also granted appointment letters to more than 20 special advisors of the center, including Wolfgang Kubin, Alexsei Rodionov, Liu Heping, and Lu Jiande, and took photos with them. All the special advisors are renowned sinologists, translators, or scholars. They thanked China Publishing Group Corp. and China Translation Research Center for having them on the advisory board and promised to make good use of their respective expertise to make due contribution to the center.

China Translation Research Center is aimed to fully mobilize and pool translation resources of China; build a platform for the communication and cooperation between Chinese and foreign translators, sinologists, publishers, translation institutions, foreign language universities, and various media agencies; carry out research on cultural communication and translation; promote relevant practices and international exchanges; provide guiding opinions to help the translation and publishing industry go global; and promote diversified and healthy development of the industry.


According to Lin Guofu, Secretary General of China Translation Research Center and General Manager of China Translation & Publishing Corporation, China Translation Research Center mainly has the following six tasks: 1) Do research on major issues and trends of translation services and cultural publishing & communication; 2) Focus on young overseas sinologists, and literature translators, explore overseas translators especially for less-spoken languages, and create a platform for collaboration and communication: 3) Build a platform for literature translation and international communication and cooperation so as to promote the going global of Chinese culture, and bring in foreign cultures; 4）Forge a platform that gather language service resources and developers of innovative technologies, make full use of the Internet, cloud computing, and big data to enhance the overall development level of the industry; 5) Create the largest basic multilingual corpse and parallel text database of China to provide fundamental support for the industry; 6) Build an international e-commerce platform and an open platform for language talent training and evaluation.


Eric Yu, Executive Deputy Secretary General of China Translation Research Center and Deputy General Manager of China Translation & Publishing Corporation, said that China Translation Research Center is dedicated to building a platform for international communication and cooperation in the fields of cultural communication and translation and promoting diversified and healthy development of the translation and publishing industries. He also released a bluebook, Report on the Development Trend of the Global Language Service Industry.


According to an insider, following the seminar on translation talent cultivation and gathering, China Translation Research Center will next launch the sinologist training program together with Beijing Language and Culture University. Then at the end of the year, it will launch the Cloud Translation Platform Entrepreneurship Contest and the experiment project jointly created with higher education institutions for speech recognition.
