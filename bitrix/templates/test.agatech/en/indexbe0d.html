China Key Words Launched in Beijing

On August 23, 2015, China Translation Research Center under China Publishing Group launched China Key Words and its official website (www.cnkeywords.net) at the 2nd International Communication Forum on Chinese Culture. China Key Words covers 12 categories including politics, economy, science and technology, and culture, and incorporates one million entries in 17 languages. It gives an authoritative presentation of China to the outside world in a brand new perspective and disseminates China's voice worldwide.

Official website of China Key Words

China Publishing Group and China Translation Research Center bring together the authoritative wisdom of senior translators and sinologists from all over the world and make use of the most advanced big data and semantic analysis & search technologies to present a full picture of the keys words of China and how they came into being over time. Global users may look through core words along a timeline to feel the dynamics of China in a more vivid way.


Ambassador Xu Yanan launching China Key Words


China Key Words gathers words, knowledge and information that contain the rich Chinese wisdom and the essence of the Chinese culture. But it does not display these in simple entries of words and phrases. Instead, the website provides sentences, explanations, passages, and recommended publications and videos to present extensive and professional interpretations in a more comprehensive and acceptable way, leaving more vivid impressions.


At the event


According to an expert of China Translation Research Center, in the new era, we should take a new perspective to share and communicate China's development and the Chinese culture and in this process, a major concern is how to make full use of the Internet/mobile Internet and big data technology to help with the communication of the Chinese culture.

[About China Translation Research Center]

Founded in August 2014, China Translation Research Center is a platform created by China Publishing Group by pooling resources from home and abroad for international exchanges and cooperation in the fields of cultural communication and translation. The Center aims to fully mobilize and integrate translation resources in China, set up a platform for communication between Chinese and foreign literary translators, sinologists, publishing enterprises, translation institutions, foreign language colleges and media agencies; promote exchanges and cooperation; carry out such activities as research, practice and international exchanges in the field of cultural communication and translation; provide guidance for the going global campaign of the translation and publishing industries; and promote the healthy development and diversification of the industries.
