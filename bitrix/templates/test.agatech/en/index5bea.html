2019 Sinobo Guoan Cup International Youth Football Development Forum: LanguageBox provides AI language-technology services

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 11 June 2019views:18

On June 8, 2019, the 2019 Sinobo Guoan Cup International Youth Football Development Forum was held in Beijing. Six clubs from Europe and America shared their youth football training concepts and experiences and signed Youth Football Training Friendship Memorandums with Beijing Sinobo Guoan F.C. and Hebei Huaxia Fortune F.C.LanguageBox, GTCOM's smart conference black technology, supported the forum by providing machine translation, smart note-taking and other AI language-technology services, representing another scenario-based application of cutting-edge AI technology in the field of sports.


▲2019 Sinobo Guoan Cup International Youth Football Development Forum held in Beijing

The forum was held during the first Sinobo Guoan Cup International Youth Invitational Tournament of the Beijing Sinobo Guoan F.C. Representatives from PSV Eindhoven, Cruzeiro Esporte Clube, Wolverhampton Wanderers F.C., Boca Juniors, SK Slavia Praha and Nacional Montevideo discussed the important factors of the youth football training system--particularly scouting, education and sports performance--and cited the corresponding evaluation criteria. Li Shubin, the director of Youth Football Training of the Chinese Football Association (CFA), revealed that the CFA is committed to further developing the youth football training system of Chinese football in addition to the youth footfall development at the club level. GTCOM, through its LanguageBox intelligent hardware, provided AI-empowered language-technology services for sports authority leaders, experts and club representatives and helped them communicate smoothly.


▲LanguageBox provides AI language-technology services for the forum


As an international event intended for the exchange of information and experiences, the forum requires quick, concise communication of the participants' ideas as well as the full exchange and discussion of their own experiences. LanguageBox ensured an excellent communication experience for the guests through three major functions: AI simultaneous interpreting, smart note-taking and mobile conference room, supporting the exchange and promotion of international youth football training using leading AI technologies. Based on the management of technical terms and the training capability of corpus data in professional fields, LanguageBox can intelligently identify terms in speeches and automatically translate them into technical terms, thus maximizing the accuracy of machine-translation results in fields of professional endeavor. In addition to sports, LanguageBox can provide AI language-technology services in different vertical fields such as finance, technology and healthcare.

The newest generation of information technologies represented by big data, artificial intelligence and cloud computing are being applied to the sports industry which will usher in new changes. By providing language-technology solutions for this forum, LanguageBox added another scenario-based application of cutting-edge AI technology in the field of sports. As a technology company dedicated to big data, artificial intelligence and language technology, GTCOM will continue to explore AI frontier technologies and conduct more explorations and attempts in the fields of finance, technology and sports, thereby facilitating the development of vertical industries.
