GTCOM's language technology supports the China-ASEAN Workshop on Big-Data Technology and Industry Development; VP Elena Zhang discusses cross-language big data and value mining powered by AI

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 23 November 2018views:98

With the strong support of the Chinese government, ASEAN governments and embassies in China, the China-ASEAN Workshop on Big-Data Technology and Industry Development hosted by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the CICS-CERT was held in Beijing from November 20 to 22, 2018. As the exclusive language-technology service provider of the workshop, Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM) demonstrated its strengths in AI big data and language technology. Elena Zhang, the vice president of GTCOM, was invited to the conference and gave a speech titled "Central Enterprises Against the Background of the Digital Silk Road: AI Empowers Cross-Language Big Data and Value Mining," sharing GTCOM's attempts and explorations in the field of AI big data.


China-ASEAN Workshop on Big-Data Technology and Industry Development held in Beijing

China-ASEAN Workshop on Big-Data Technology and Industry Development

Digital Silk Road Construction Matchmaking Meeting

Ms. Zhang, in her speech at the Digital Silk Road Construction Matchmaking Meeting of the Workshop, shared the company's layout and exploration of AI big data with Chinese and foreign guests with example of the company's latest-technology products. "As a company dedicated to big data, artificial intelligence and language technology, GTCOM has constantly endeavored to develop the level of AI that would enable cross-language big data and value mining. From the launch of the Global Call Center in 2013 to the release of the Multi-Language Machine Translation Platform, from the proposal of the global cross-language big-data concept to the establishment of the 2020 Cognitive Intelligence Research Institute, GTCOM has established in-depth layout in the industry segments of big data and artificial intelligence, and we are accelerating our evolution through cutting-edge technology," Ms. Zhang said.


Elena Zhang, the vice president of GTCOM

GTCOM has built a cross-language big-data analysis and processing platform in the field of big data, doing so with completely independent intellectual property rights, providing customized solutions for different industries such as finance, technology, industry and smart city. In her speech, Ms. Zhang introduced the latest achievements of GTCOM in AI big-data applications by examples of AI big-data products such as the JoveEye technology big-data platform, the JoveBird financial big-data platform, the JoveMind knowledge graph building and analysis platform, etc. Additionally, in the field of language technology, GTCOM has continued to make breakthroughs. Ms. Zhang introduced GTCOM's explorations and attempts in language technology with the example of such machine-translation products as the MerCube enterprise-level all-in-one artificial intelligence machine, the LanguageBox conference machine-translation system, document translation, YeeCaption and the JoveTrans artificial intelligence translation machine. The leaders, experts and scholars from the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the CICS-CERT and well-known technology companies as well as officials and experts from Thailand, Singapore, Myanmar, Indonesia, Cambodia, Laos, Vietnam, the Philippines, Brunei and other countries experienced the convenience that have made GTCOM's language-technology products so useful in exchange, discussion and study.


GTCOM's language-technology products provide services for the workshop

GTCOM's LanguageBox conference machine-translation system showed off its strength at the discussions and communications throughout the three-day workshop, particularly in regard to the themes of the "Belt and Road" Intelligent Manufacturing Future Cooperation Driven by Industrial Big Data, the Digital Silk Road Construction Matchmaking Meeting, and the Industrial Information Security Development Situation and Direction, providing language-technology services throughout the whole process, which received high praise from everyone present. During exchanges with the Yongyou Group and SenseTime, GTCOM's JoveTrans artificial intelligence translation machine provided professional, convenient, efficient language-technology services for Chinese and foreign guests. Efficient, high-quality professional language-technology services were lauded by the Chinese and foreign guests.


China and ASEAN, linked by mountains and rivers, are good neighbors, good friends and good partners. As mentioned in the China-ASEAN Strategic Partnership Vision 2030, China and ASEAN will explore the opportunities brought forth by new technologies and digital/technological innovations, and address the potential common challenges of new technologies to economic development. This China-ASEAN Workshop on Big-Data Technology and Industry Development epitomized joint efforts of multiple parties to embrace the opportunities and challenges inherent in the development of new-generation information technology. In the future, as a technology company dedicated to big data, artificial intelligence and language technology, GTCOM will capitalize on its strengths as it embodies the responsibility and technical strength of a Chinese technology company in helping pave the Digital Silk Road.
