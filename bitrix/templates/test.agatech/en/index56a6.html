YeeCaption, a one-stop solution to video subtitle editing and translation.

Smart subtitle translation software


Anyone familiar with subtitle translation knows that it takes at least 6 minutes to finish dictation, timeline cutting, translation, proofreading and subtitle compression for a one-minute video. With the development of new-generation information technologies, video subtitle production is getting smarter.Today, I'll introduce a one-stop subtitle translation tool designed by AI big-data technology, which can generate bilingual subtitles intelligently in a few seconds for a 1-minute video. What is the origin of this powerful subtitle translation tool?First let's take a look at its basic information.

About YeeCaption

Chinese name:字幕通

English name:YeeCaption

Invented by:GTCOM (a technology company dedicated to big data and artificial intelligence)

Occupation:One-stop smart subtitle translation software

Features:Real-time speech recognition; fast, automatic timeline cutting; smart, efficient machine translation; accurate manual proofreading; and team task collaboration

Certificate:By the end of May 2019, it has obtained five patents, including subtitle matching, subtitle index, subtitle restoration, automatic production of film and television timeline, and automatic production of video timeline of lectures as well as 1 software copyright, and more than 30 patents related to machine translation are under application.


Learning experience:Machine translation, natural language processing, timeline cutting, and analysis and processing of specialized corpus


Related fields:Big data, artificial intelligence, machine translation, speech recognition

Self-introduction:I'm the smart video subtitle editing and translation software developed by GTCOM.I can provide one-stop video localization solutions such as automatic timeline cutting, text recognition, smart subtitle translation and finished product exporting.I also support manual timeline editing, proofreading of original translation, subtitle task collaboration and other operations. My mission is to make video subtitle production and translation more intelligent, convenient and efficient.

Through the above introduction, are you interested in YeeCaption?






The software, with a simple interface, is powerful but user-friendly. With only five steps, you can complete the production of intelligent bilingual subtitles and bilingual videos.


✔ Step 1: Log in and import the local video.


✔ Step 2: After the video is loaded, select the original language and target language. The system currently supports Chinese, English, French, Japanese, German and other common languages.


✔ Step 3: Click "One-Stop" to complete automatic timeline cutting, text recognition, machine translation, subtitle compression and other operations.





✔ Step 4: In the subtitle translation editing area, manually edit and proofread bilingual subtitles.


YeeCaption also has "voice timeline cutting," "subtitle timeline cutting," "slapping mode" and other functions.



You can manually adjust the timeline according to your needs, select the starting and ending points of the timeline, and review the original text and translation so as to ensure accurate translation with maximum efficiency.

If there is a ready-made subtitle file, click "Import Subtitles" and then select "Automatic Translation" to generate bilingual subtitles intelligently for convenient manual review.


✔ Step 5: Complete production of bilingual subtitles and customize the subtitle display style. The software supports multi-format original/translation/bilingual subtitle files and one-click video exporting.


Major audio and video file formats including .avi, .mp4, .flv, .rmvb and .f4v, are acceptable.



With YeeCaption, subtitle dictation, translation, timeline cutting, compression and other core problems are readily solved.Act now to buy this remarkable software. It can liberate the subtitle translator from tedious manual work.
