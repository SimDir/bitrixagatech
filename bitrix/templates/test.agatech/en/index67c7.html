Company Brief

GTCOM Digital Media and Entertainment Co., Ltd. is a wholly owned subsidiary of Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. Through comprehensive use of language technology, big data, artificial intelligence and other leading industrial technologies, GTCOM Digital Media and Entertainment draws from a deep pool of high-quality IP and related industrial resources. Its mission is to build an innovative digital media and entertainment technology company committed to the use of AI for a more entertaining life based on an international presence, copyright and technology.
