Shanghai Deputy Mayor Xu Kunlin and delegation visit Shibei High-Tech Park Enterprise AI Experience Pavilion and hear GTCOM's report on AI big data

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 30 November 2018views:100

Xu Kunlin, the deputy mayor of Shanghai, together with Gu Jinshan, deputy secretary general of the Shanghai Municipal Government, Lu Xiaodong, district governor and secretary of the Jing'an District Party Committee, and leaders from the Shanghai Administration for Industry and Commerce, the Shanghai Municipal Commission of Commerce, the Shanghai Science and Technology Committee, the Shanghai Financial Supervision Bureau, the Shanghai Municipal Commission of Economy and Informatization, and the Shanghai Municipal Development & Reform Commission visited the Shanghai Shibei High-Tech Park Enterprise AI Experience Pavilion on November 28, 2018, where they listened to GTCOM's report on its results of machine translation and AI big data.

Xu Kunlin, deputy mayor of Shanghai, and his delegation visits the Shibei High-Tech Park Enterprise AI Experience Pavilion

Leaders of the Shanghai Municipal Government, responding to the state's call to strengthen support for high-tech enterprises, have attached great importance to the development of artificial intelligence technology and industry as they endeavored to build a "Shanghai highland" for the development of artificial intelligence. During the visit, Mr. Xu and his delegation visited GTCOM's exhibition area and watched the company's demonstration of innovative achievements. Later, in the workshop context, GTCOM Shanghai General Manager Jiang Wei reported to Mr. Xu and his delegation on the company's achievements in the fields of big data, artificial intelligence and language technology.


Mr. Xu and his delegation visit GTCOM's exhibition area

GTCOM's booth at the Shibei High-Tech Park Enterprise AI Experience Pavilion

Mr. Xu emphasized he importance of business development, talent introduction, intellectual property protection and a fair market environment in the process of enterprise innovation and development. In his talk during the workshop, he said that all departments in the city should create a fair and sound environment for the development of high-tech innovation enterprises, support policies and enterprises, and help such enterprises solve practical difficulties so that they can concentrate on sound development. He conveyed the hope that high-tech AI enterprises would constantly elevate their technological capabilities, redouble their efforts in regard to innovation and strive to enhance their core competitiveness. He added that enterprises should offer development suggestions in regard to industrial trends, directions and standards based on their own best practices.


Mr. Xu attends the workshop and delivers a speech

Jiang Wei, general manager of GTCOM Shanghai,

delivers a report on the company's results in machine translation and AI big data


Workshop site


The ever-accelerating trend of innovative breakthroughs, with its integrated applications of new-generation information technology, has become the means for countries around the world to seize historic opportunities and ensure development. Therefore, GTCOM will continue to focus on innovations in the application of artificial intelligence and big data, contribute more self-developed solutions that will promote the development of artificial intelligence and big-data industries, and empower the business future with the technology of AI. In doing so, it will rely on Shanghai's sound development environment and policy support for artificial intelligence industry.
