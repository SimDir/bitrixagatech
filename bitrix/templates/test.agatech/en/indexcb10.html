GTCOM presents AI big-data and language-technology solutions at the China Beijing International Fair for Trade in Services 2019

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 31 May 2019views:36

The China Beijing International Fair for Trade in Services 2019 (CIFTIS) was held in Beijing during the period of May 28 to June 1, 2019, with sponsorship by the Ministry of Commerce of the People's Republic of China and the Beijing Municipal People's Government. As the world's first international exchange platform dedicated to trade in services, the conference drew participants from 137 countries and regions, including 86 countries and regions as hosts of exhibits and conferences. GTCOM brought its epochal AI big-data products to the CIFTIS event and provided high-quality language-technology solutions for a range of activities.

China Beijing International Fair for Trade in Services 2019 held in Beijing

It was inaugural event held under the new name- CIFTIS- and was based on a theme of "openness, innovation, wisdom and integration." The conference had one main session at the China National Convention Center and the surrounding Olympic Celebration Square and Beijing International Convention Center in addition to 10 parallel sessions at Wangfujing Commercial Street, Xicheng Financial Street, China International Exhibition Center Shunyi New Hall, Chaoyang CBD and others. Thus, there was a total exhibition area of 165,000 square meters. GTCOM presented the latest AI big-data products at the main session as well as parallel sessions at Chaoyang CBD and China International Exhibition Center Shunyi New Hall, at which it displayed the company's groundbreaking achievements in the exploration and scenario-based application of big data, artificial intelligence and language technology.

GTCOM, as an excellent intelligent technology enterprise in Beijing,

displayed its products at the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei exhibition area of the main session

At the China National Convention Center, GTCOM, with its status as an outstanding intelligent technology enterprise in Beijing, was highlighted in the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei exhibition area, where the company showcased its language-technology products and AI big-data solutions to guests and exhibitors from cities around the world. Additionally, GTCOM provided smart conference services for "Beijing Theme Day" via its proprietary LanguageBox system.

GTCOM's exhibit in the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei exhibition area

Visitors were able to experience the three core functions of LanguageBox- machine translation, smart note taking and mobile office- each of which brought high praise. Additionally, GTCOM's JoveMind knowledge-graph building and analysis platform, JoveBird Financial Big Data, DataMap data visualized analysis platform and other AI big-data solutions garnered enviable attention.


JoveEye Technology Big Data Platform

presented in the Intellectual Property Services exhibition area of the main venue

JoveEye Technology Big Data Platform is the center of attention

At the Intellectual Property Services exhibition area of the main venue, GTCOM's JoveEye Technology Big Data Platform, as the only AI big-data platform in the vicinity, was a subject of considerable interest. Long Mingtao, the director of the Technology Big Data Business Group, introduced the various functions of JoveEye and the industrial application solutions it could provide, doing so through descriptions of practical examples. The guests expressed their praise and recognition for the platform along with their eagerness to apply for trial use.

LanguageBox provides language-technology services for the China International Convention and Exhibition Industry Development Conference


The China International Convention and Exhibition Industry Development Conference and the Beijing Convention and Exhibition Economic Forum, as important parts of CIFTIS, were held at the Crowne Plaza Beijing International Airport Hotel. GTCOM provided language-technology solutions to guarantee the success of the conference. Through the use of LanguageBox, the guests' speeches were presented on the screen in real time, thus enhancing their ease of understanding salient points. Thus, the system facilitates the scenario-based application of AI big-data technology in the convention and exhibition service industry. LanguageBox was created to make meetings smarter.

GTCOM provides language-technology solutions for the CIFTIS series activities


Additionally, GTCOM's language-technology solutions supported the parallel session at Chaoyang CBD by providing superb language-technology services for the guests and thereby guaranteeing a successful communication experience for all who attended.


From the main session at the China National Convention Center to the parallel sessions at the China International Exhibition Center Shunyi New Hall and Chaoyang CBD, GTCOM's personnel in charge of the various product lines coordinated all matters for the 2019 CIFTIS. GTCOM is committed to promoting the global exchange of China's new business opportunities using AI big-data solutions. In the future, through reliance on GTCOM's technological advantages and resource integration capabilities, the company will accelerate the new development process of global trade in services and commit itself to enabling more enterprise users to board the express train toward fluency in AI.
