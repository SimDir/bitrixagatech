Leaders of the Department of Language Information Management under the Ministry of Education visit GTCOM

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 21 November 2017views:306

Ms. Tian Lixin, the director of the Department of Language Application and Administration and the Department of Language Information Management under the Ministry of Education, and Wang Qi, the head of the Planning and Coordination Division of the Department of Language Information Management, visited GTCOM on November 21, 2017. CEO Eric Yu and Vice President Elena Zhang conducted in-depth exchanges on topics such as the innovation and development of language-information technology and the model for cooperation between enterprises and the government.


Tian Lixin, the director of the Department of Language Application and Administration and the Department of Language Information Management under the Ministry of Education



Eric Yu, CEO of GTCOM


GTCOM CEO Eric Yu introduced the company's achievements in the research and development of technology systems such as speech recognition, natural-language processing, big-data analysis, machine translation, vertical-area semantic search, neural network deep learning and artificial intelligence. He demonstrated the YeeKit CAT, YeeCaption, the Japanese pronunciation-correction system and other language technology applications under the YeeCloud language technology and the YeeSight big-data ecosystems, as well as industry big-data solutions oriented towards enterprise-level users such as government, media, education, finance, industry, healthcare and other vertical areas.

Eric Yu also mentioned that GTCOM established the Language Big Data Alliance (LBDA) in 2016. LBDA is a global, multi-field, multilingual cooperation platform based on open, shared big-data resources, aiming at governments, industries, universities and research institutes. Relying on LBDA, members can jointly build language and big-data teaching laboratories, and complete horizontal projects such as research project applications, entrepreneurship and innovation projects, and internship practice, in order to achieve synergies across different disciplines with data that supports theories. At present, LBDA has 181 universities and institutions from all over the world. It established the Chinese-Portuguese-English Machine Translation Laboratory together with the Macao Polytechnic Institute and the Guangdong University of Foreign Studies, and built the National Communication Strategy Research Center jointly with the School of Journalism and Communication at Renmin University of China.

Director Tian Lixin expressed great appreciation for GTCOM's encouraging results in machine translation, semantic analysis, video speech recognition and in-depth mining of the application value of cross-language big data through research and development. She pointed out that, in July of this year, the State Council issued the "New-Generation Artificial Intelligence Development Plan," which expresses clear objectives for key common technologies intended to achieve breakthroughs in natural-language processing technology. "Artificial intelligence is a strategic technology that leads the future. Its rapid development will profoundly change human's social life as well as the world. The intelligent understanding and automatic generation of natural language, as well as the in-depth study of cross-language text mining technology and machine cognition-based semantic understanding technology, will be essential for promoting effective communication and free interaction between humans and machines. We must seize the major strategic opportunities for the development of artificial intelligence, focus on development, take the initiative to plan and lead the new trend of artificial intelligence development, thus serving the economic and social development and supporting national security."

Director Tian stressed that language is a fundamental, overall, social and national cause. He added that it's essential as a supporting force for the country's comprehensive strength, playing an important role in the national development strategy. According to the "13th Five-Year Plan for Development of National Language as a Cause," the State Language Commission will play a leading coordinative role in promoting the innovation and development of language-information technology, supporting the promotion of natural-language processing, speech recognition and synthesis, text recognition, and other intelligent theory research and technology development, supporting the study of machine translation between different languages and the development of technology products for intelligent assisted learning and evaluation of national common language while giving play to the basic role of language information application in underpinning the building of national informatization. It is therefore expected that GTCOM will align its development with the needs of the country. The government will establish a platform whose fulfillment the company can then undertake. GTCOM is also expected to achieve breakthroughs in and make greater contributions to the research and development of key technologies for language-information processing, information application in education and the inheritance of excellent traditional Chinese culture.


Site of the meeting


The two sides will deepen cooperation in the application of language technology and big-data technology in the fields of education information building and cultural inheritance.
