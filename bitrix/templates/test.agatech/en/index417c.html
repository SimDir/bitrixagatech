LanguageBox presented at the Pujiang Innovation Forum 2019 empowered by AI language technology

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 27 May 2019views:30

The Pujiang Innovation Forum 2019 was held in Shanghai from May 24 to the 26, 2019, with joint sponsorship by the Ministry of Science and Technology of the People's Republic of China and the Shanghai Municipal People's Government. Government officials, experts, scholars and entrepreneurs from 23 countries and regions gathered to discuss the latest trends in technology and collectively generate a new vision for global technology innovation. As the AI language-technology partner at the forum, GTCOM (Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd.) provided leading AI language-technology solutions for the forum using LanguageBox--a "black" technology for smart conferencing--thus helping this edition of the forum attain a new level of efficiency.


Pujiang Innovation Forum 2019 held in Shanghai

GTCOM attends the Pujiang Innovation Forum as the official AI language-technology partner

AI empowers Technology Davos

The Pujiang Innovation Forum, founded in 2008, is a high-level international forum jointly created by the Ministry of Science and Technology and the Shanghai municipal government. Using the mode of "Technology Davos," the Pujiang Innovation Forum 2019 is committed to building a global technology innovation summit with international influence. This forum included 1 opening ceremony and general assembly, 4 special forums and 11 thematic forums, and foreign guests accounted for about one third. In this international technology forum, GTCOM, using the LanguageBox intelligent hardware, provided AI language-technology services for top experts and scholars from key centers of technology worldwide, thus helping make communication smooth and efficient.

LanguageBox gets the attention of the guests

LanguageBox, which integrates AI simultaneous interpretation, smart note-taking and mobile conference room, not only helped the guests to quickly capture and understand the core views and contents of speakers but also improved the working efficiency of data processing staff. According to a technical person of GTCOM, LanguageBox facilitates real-time translation in 37 languages, including Chinese, English, Japanese, German, Portuguese, French and Korean, in speech, multilingual exchange, dialogue and subtitling modes. It has industry leading strength in both accuracy and fluency. Smart note-taking has a unique advantage in improving productivity. No matter it is a long speech or a several-hour multilingual meeting, audio and text files can be generated intelligently, and file exportation is supported. Moreover, mobile conference function makes remote conversation and communication easier. By scanning the QR code of a meeting, people who are not present at the meeting may know the content of the meeting in real time through smart terminals such as mobile phones. Thanks to LanguageBox, international conference communication has become a powerful tool for progress.


Language technology breaks down communication barriers in the S&T finance industry


GTCOM, which has previously served at the Internet Security Conference (ISC) and the China International Software Expo, has gained valuable experience in serving large multinational financial conferences using LanguageBox as a field-based, intelligent simultaneous interpreting device. During the Pujiang Innovation Forum 2019, LanguageBox provided a full range of AI language-technology services for the Technology Finance Forum, the Regional (City) Forum, the Cultural Forum and other sessions, and in every case those services were praised by the guests and industry users.


LanguageBox provides AI language-technology services for the Technology Finance Forum,

the Regional (City) Forum, the Cultural Forum and other sessions


International conferences such as the Technology Finance Forum, where guests from home and abroad gathered to discuss the requirements for flexible multilingual translation, as well as the Technology Finance Forum, were characterized by sophisticated terminologies. Faced with professional terms in technology and finance fields such as Science and Technology Innovation Board, big data credit reference, financial risk, inclusive finance, digital economy and Internet finance, LanguageBox is based on GTCOM's neural network machine-translation engine for in-depth learning of corpus in technology and finance fields, so as to create an efficient and professional communication experience for the guests at a conference with real-time multilingual translation and real-time subtitling. GTCOM's technical staff, on hand at the event, said the current LanguageBox-based terminology-management function delivers more targeted professional language-technology services for different vertical fields, including finance, technology, healthcare and sports.

The AI simultaneous interpreting service made possible by LanguageBox breaks down the barriers between languages, and its terminology-management function breaks the communication barriers of different industries, so that people in vertical fields to benefit from more professional language-technology services.
