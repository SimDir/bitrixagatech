Language services step into the "new era": GTCOM leads the industry development summit

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 7 November 2017views:261

On November 4 and 5, Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. (GTCOM), as the main sponsor, attended the New Era New Model: 2017 China Translation Service Industry Development Summit hosted by the Translation Service Committee of the Translators Association of China in Wenchang, Hainan. Huang Song, executive director of the Translators Association of China, director of the Translation Service Committee and general manager of China Translation Corporation, addressed the conference. Elisa Chai, a vice president at GTCOM, delivered a keynote speech.



Huang Song, executive director of the Translators Association of China, director of the Translation Service Committee and general manager of China Translation Corporation, addresses the conference


Huang Song stated that the language service has risen to the national strategic level and that the building of the language-service system has brought new opportunities and challenges. Reform and innovation have become a new trend in the language-service industry in the new era. In particular, the development of big data and AI has given powerful impetus to the entire industry. He hoped that this conference will promote the building of the language-service system and advance the integration of language services and technologies, in order to create a new landscape for the language industry in the new era.


Zhong Weihe, Executive Vice Chairman of the Translators Association of China and Co-President of KINGOLD Group, delivers a keynote speech中国


Zhong Weihe, Executive Vice Chairman[CC1]of the Translators Association of China and Co-President of KINGOLD Group, was invited to deliver a keynote speech. He analyzed the status quo of domestic language-service research, and pointed out that the language-service industry currently has such problems as unclear status and not complete discipline system. He proposed to establish macro language-service science and micro-language-service science, draw lessons from foreign advanced experience, and pay attention to the transformation of the talent training model, with a view to vigorously promoting the prosperity and development of language-service research amid the ever-changing landscape of technology.


GTCOM Vice President Elisa Chai delivers a keynote speech

Ms. Chai, in her keynote speech titled "Language Service Providers in the AI Era," noted that the language-service industry has entered an era marked by new technologies such as big data and AI. The core resources of language-service providers include not only customer resources but also those of technology, language assets and collaboration. Driven by new technologies, developments with respect to platforms, online modes and integration will become active trends in the language-service industry, thus highlighting the differentiation and diversification of language products.


GTCOM strives to build a new language-capability platform that integrates core products such as human translation, machine translation, speech recognition and collaboration management in order to tap the online value of data. By integrating language technologies as a core measure, GTCOM will sharpen the direction of the language-service industry, promote the penetration of language services into all industries and achieve comprehensive development.


At the case-sharing session of the subforum on "Standardized Services and Applications of the Translation Service Industry," Song Weiming, the director of the GTCOM Language Business Group's Marketing Center, described the important role of the document "Specification for Translation Services" in regulating business operations and orienting customers as well as GTCOM's application of language-service practices. Additionally, he made recommendations with respect to the development framework as the means to adapt to the scope of application of national standards amid the new era and emerging demands. At the subforum "Translation Technology Development and Application," Ms. Shi Simei, the director of GTCOM's Localization Department, systematically introduced the various technologies and application scenarios involved in the translation business from the perspective of industrial integration driven by language technology. She also described GTCOM's independently developed YeeKit platform in a manner that highlighted the company's impressive technical strength. Ms. Shi called for win-win cooperation and common development among the industry counterparts. Additionally, Product Manager Song Yuheng introduced the various offerings and platform-level services in the YeeCloud language technology and YeeSight big-data ecosystem, doing so from the perspectives of technology innovation, big-data applications and business models.

The summit was one in which GTCOM again demonstrated its capabilities and potential while establishing its benchmarking position. Thus the company will continue to uphold the corporate attitude of resource sharing and win-win cooperation as it provides global users with a comprehensive "one-stop" linguistic experience through integrated industry output based on technology, applications and services, thereby building an industry-leading ecosystem for the smart business of today and tomorrow.
