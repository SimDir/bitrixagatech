GTCOM attends 2018 Annual Conference of the China Big Data Technology and Application Alliance; VP Elena Zhang shares experiences in AI

NewsSource:GTCOMDate: 10 December 2018views:119

The 2018 Big Data Technology and Application Seminar & Annual Conference of the China Big Data Technology and Application Alliance under the banner "Data Empowering Potential of Mining Industry Talents" was held in Beijing on December 8, 2018. More than 300 leaders, experts and scholars from the industry gathered to share the development innovation and application practices of AI big data as well as to discuss the industrial demand and development direction. GTCOM Vice President Elena Zhang attended the gathering, at which she shared the company's innovative explorations and attempts in scenario-based application of AI in a speech titled "Global Knowledge Platform Supported by Language Technology."

The 2018 Annual Conference of the China Big Data Technology and Application Alliance held in Beijing

Data-driven artificial intelligence is developing rapidly with the deepening of the information revolution. The vast amount of global data is being generated in various forms and used in the Internet, communications, electronics, healthcare, finance and other sectors. Worldwide, the ability of large-scale data processing and analysis has become the standard by which to gauge core competitiveness. GTCOM, as the world's leading big-data and artificial intelligence company, has explored a path of innovation and development in the era of AI big data.

Ms. Zhang said, "From the Internet to big data and artificial intelligence, GTCOM has explored the potential of global massive big data through innovative practices, thus realizing various scenario-based applications of AI big data." Thus, she introduced the development history of the company, describing the explorations and achievements in cross-language big data, AI language technology and other aspects.

Elena Zhang, vice president of GTCOM

Ms. Zhang used MerCube MT--the world's first enterprise-level machine-translation hardware--as an example of GTCOM's cutting-edge exploration in the field of AI language technology. She explained that MerCube MT can seamlessly interface with software such as webpage translation, subtitle translation, image translation and video translation. With a precise, extremely stable operating environment, excellent translation capability and an innovative machine-translation model, it leads the way in AI enterprise application. Ms. Zhang also introduced GTCOM's language-technology achievements, including the LanguageBox conference machine-translation system and the JoveTrans artificial intelligence translation machine, which supports major conferences at home and abroad with high-quality language technology services.


"With the accumulation in big data and artificial intelligence, GTCOM has focused on technology big data, financial big data and knowledge-graph applications since 2018," Ms. Zhang said. In the field of cross-language big data, she highlighted AI big-data products such as the JoveEye technology big-data platform and the JoveMind knowledge-graph building and analysis platform. JoveEye Research Big Data embodies GTCOM's effort to apply AI cross-language big data in research and innovation. JoveEye, based on GTCOM's exclusive cross-language big-data analysis technology, analyzes the vast amount of scientific and technological data in the world through exclusive algorithms and models such as cross-language full-text semantic retrieval, hype-cycle curve, patent-cluster map and value analysis to produce highly valuable results. Ms. Zhang noted that JoveEye Research Big Data has mastered the most challenging contemporary research problem while providing knowledge services for scholars, enterprise researchers, intellectual property professionals and other users of scientific information. When talking about the application of the JoveMind knowledge graph building and analysis platform, Ms. Zhang used a specific enterprise as an example and introduced its powerful function of helping the enterprise deeply depict enterprise relations and business layout while analyzing security status and risks.


Conference site

The continuous development of new-generation information technology, the mature application of cloud computing, big data, the Internet of Things, artificial intelligence and other new technologies have profoundly changed people's work and life. Moreover, they have accelerated the optimization of the economic structure and industrial upgrading. In the future, GTCOM will continue to seek innovation in technologies such as big data, artificial intelligence and language technology as it empowers industrial upgrading and development with AI big data in order to provide global users with AI big-data solutions and scenario-based application products blessed with the enviable GTCOM characteristics.
