Company Brief

Established on July 25, 2017, GTCOM Chengdu serves as an important part of GTCOM’s strategic move to expand its market presence in Southwest China and even in the global, and will contribute the growth of the company in the cross-language big data and AI industry.  

In January 2017, GTCOM Chengdu reached a strategic partnership with the Business School of Sichuan University and The Cover, jointly established the CG Big Data Research Center, and released the Global Cover Index (CGI). The CGI is the first data index in China to combine global open big data, leading AI algorithm technology and professional consulting analysis under the guidance of respected academic institutions, and will provide big data-based professional consultation and suggestions for varieties of industries in Sichuan.  

GTCOM Chengdu has now expanded businesses to civil-military integration technologies, aviation, finance, culture and other fields, and has carried out in-depth cooperation with well-known organizations such as Chengdu High-tech Zone, Guobao Life Insurance, Sichuan Daily Newspaper Group, China Academy of Engineering Physics, BBD, etc.
