Company Brief

Global Tone Communication Technology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd. (GTCOM Shanghai or the Company) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Global Tone Communication Technology Co., Ltd. It is a leading language and Big Data analysis service provider in China. Combining its expertise in languages with Big Data and AI technology, GTCOM Shanghai is committed to providing governments, enterprises and individual users with customized language services, such as translation and interpretation, machine translation, Big Data analysis, as well as with Big Data solutions for industries. We are the world-class language service provider for several international events including the 2010 Shanghai World Expo, the 2014 Nanjing Youth Olympic Games, the 2015 World Table Tennis Championships, the Shanghai International Film Festival, and for the upcoming 2018 China International Import Expo, as well as for large corporate meetings in different industries. We have also established a strategic cooperation with China Unicom Shanghai and several other companies to tap into and leverage the application value of language-based Big Data.
